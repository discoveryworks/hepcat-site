Half your brain is listening.
The other half wants music.

Podcasts feed the side of you that wants words, ideas, conversation. But the other side sits in silence. HEPCAT is a podcast player with a house band — a live layer of generative music, mixed under the talk, so both sides of your head get what they came for.

A real player

Search any public podcast, subscribe, download for offline, play at your speed. Lock screen, background audio, the works. No account, no feed algorithm.

A house band

A generative music layer starts with your episode and matches its energy — ambient for the slow talkers, upbeat for the fast ones. Wrong vibe? Hit New Vibe and the band changes tune.

It never talks over anyone

HEPCAT listens to the voices in real time and mixes the music live: swelling into the pauses, sinking under every word. Speech stays crisp. The music stays felt.
